Hello! My name is Tomo, I am currently an undergraduate at Johns Hopkins University majoring in Applied Mathematics and Statistics and Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. 

My research interest includes machine learning models for biology, specifically in the realm of single-cell and spatial omics data. Some of the projects I have worked on include multimodal interation algorithms and transformer models for single cell data. I am currently a research assistant at Johns Hopkins School of Public Health, advised by Professor Hongkai Ji.
